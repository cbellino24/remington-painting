Who We Are

Remington Affordable Outdoor Painting is a locally owned painting company serving Omaha and the surrounding areas. We were built around a simple idea: home painting should be clear, reliable, and handled without unnecessary hassle.

We focus on showing up prepared, pricing jobs honestly, and completing the work efficiently. Whether it's a single-room refresh or a full exterior repaint, our role is straightforward — protect your surfaces, apply quality coatings, and leave your property looking finished.

We work with homeowners, businesses, landlords, and property managers who want the job done correctly the first time.

What SetsUs Apart

Clear communication is a priority from the first contact through completion. We make sure you know what to expect, understand the scope of work, and receive timely updates so there's no confusion at any stage of the project.

Pricing is always provided upfront before work begins. Our estimates are straightforward and transparent, with no hidden fees or last-minute changes once the job is approved.

Our crews arrive prepared and handle all prep, masking, and cleanup. Every property is treated with care, and each job is completed thoroughly without cutting corners or leaving work unfinished.

Remington Painting is fully licensed and insured, and reliability is central to how we operate. Our goal is to complete every project efficiently and professionally, making your paint job the easiest part of your home improvement plans.
